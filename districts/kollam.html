Kollam, a picturesque coastal city in the southern Indian state of Kerala, is a hidden gem for travelers. Known as the "Cashew Capital of the World," Kollam offers serene backwaters, lush landscapes, and historic charm. Visitors can embark on houseboat journeys through the enchanting Ashtamudi Lake, explore the historic Kollam Beach, or unwind at Thangassery Lighthouse, offering breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. The city's rich cultural heritage is evident in its ancient temples, churches, and the renowned Munroe Island. With a blend of natural beauty and cultural allure, Kollam provides an authentic and tranquil escape for those seeking an off-the-beaten-path Kerala experience.
